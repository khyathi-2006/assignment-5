FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The 2014 FIFA World Cup was hosted by Brazil from June 12 to July 13, featuring 32 national teams. One of the most memorable moments was Germany’s stunning 7–1 semi-final victory over Brazil, which shocked fans worldwide. Germany went on to defeat Argentina 1–0 in the final at Maracanã Stadium, winning their fourth World Cup title. The tournament was known for its high-scoring matches, exciting play, and the emergence of stars like Colombia’s James Rodríguez, who won the Golden Boot. It also saw Miroslav Klose become the all-time top scorer in World Cup history, and marked the end of the era for several football legends.
